Welcome to Big Aquarium!

Aquariums are a window into the magical world of the ocean, where people can immerse themselves in the mysterious beauty of the sea, learn about the challenges marine life faces, and explore our efforts to protect species from extinction.

History

Over the years, the aquarium has become a symbol of the connection between humans and the ocean, providing a unique experience for millions of visitors.

Main Attractions

Highlights of the aquarium include a colorful coral reef area, a majestic shark tank and a glowing marine life zone, providing a vivid experience of the ocean world.

Notes and Regulations

Visitors are requested to comply with the rules of not touching marine life, not using flash when taking photos and maintaining general hygiene within the aquarium premises.

UNDERWATER ANIMALS!

Aquariums offer a diverse view of life under the ocean, from the colorful fish that glide through coral reefs to unique creatures like the intelligent octopus or the majestic shark.

Here, we can explore the layers of the ocean ecosystem, where each species has its own role and way of surviving in its habitat.

The marine creatures in the aquarium are not only representatives of the rich ocean ecosystem, but also a bridge to help people better understand the importance of marine conservation and protecting their natural habitat.
